Celeste Review

Summary

Celeste is a platforming video game created by Matt Makes Games.

The games stems around overcoming mistakes, as you'll die many, many, MANY, times. But this is to make your learn the in's and out's of each level given to you. Almost akin to Super Meat Boy.

In game, the backgrounds are beautiful and has amazing and unique gameplay. The main character, Madeline, begins to climb Celeste Mountain, a very deadly moutain with obstacles galore. You will meet many different people along the way, fellow climbers, old ladies, creepy charcters, including yourself.

Chapter 1: Foresaken City

The game starts you out and lets you learn the mechanics of the game. It introduces you to puzzles and one of several collectables; the strawberry.

A quote said by an old lady first met in the prolouge gives a great insight into the main charcters head. "You might see things. Things you ain't ready to see."

The Madeline repied shortly after, showing her careless attitude, "You should seek help old lady"
